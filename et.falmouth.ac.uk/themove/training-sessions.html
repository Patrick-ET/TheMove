Training Sessions

Currently Available

The updated Learning Space will be in operation from the start of the new academic year, 2013. To ensure the transition to the new system is as smooth as possible, Educational Technology is offering training sessions highlighting how best to use the new features of the Learning Space.

As well as basic 'how to' information, the sessions will also follow best practice guidelines, ensuring you are not only able to use the Learning Space, but are also able to significantly enhance students' online learning experience.

The sessions are open to any members of staff using the Learning Space, with all levels of expertise supported.
